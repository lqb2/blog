#Entry 2

6 Jan 2015

==========

lesson learned from day of reflection: one day isn’t enough. i think i also need to go away to do it properly.

if i make it to 2016, i want to take a two-day weekend or block out one full day from sunup to sundown. i was also too easily distracted during the day and was too able to find things i needed to do to prepare for diving back into the stream of life.

it’d be nice to go somewhere on friday night, probably solo, sleep there, wake up, review 2015 calendar, think about life and meaning, plan for 2016, sleep, return home the following morning.
